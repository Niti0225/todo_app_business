2) Data Types & Purposes of Processing

We only process the data necessary to provide the app’s functionality. There are no advertising trackers.

Data Types

  • Account data (email, optional name) via Firebase Authentication.
  • Content data (tasks, titles, notes, due dates) via Firestore/Storage.
  • Technical metadata (device, OS version, app version) for support and stability.
  • Purchase data (entitlements/status) via RevenueCat / App Store / Google Play.
  • Notifications (local reminders stored on your device; no push tokens collected).

Purposes

  • Providing and maintaining app functionality (tasks, calendar, focus timer).
  • Synchronizing data across devices (cloud storage).
  • Processing in-app purchases and subscription status.
  • Ensuring app security and preventing abuse.
  • Handling support requests via email.

Note Local notifications are created and triggered entirely on your device. No remote push data is sent or stored externally.

3) Legal Bases (Art. 6 para. 1 GDPR)

  • b) Contract performance – to provide the app features you use.
  • f) Legitimate interest – app security, stability, and debugging.
  • a) Consent – if you explicitly enable optional features (e.g. diagnostics). You may withdraw consent at any time with future effect.

4) Data Retention

  • Local data remains on your device until you delete it in the app or uninstall the app.
  • Cloud data is stored with your account and deleted once you delete your account or request erasure. Backups/logs may be temporarily retained for security (typically 30–90 days).
  • Support emails are retained only as long as necessary for issue resolution (typically 6–12 months) and then deleted or anonymized.

5) Recipients / Processors

We do not sell any data. Data sharing occurs only to provide the app services:

  • Google Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Storage) – account management & data storage. Privacy Info
  • RevenueCat – subscription management, without access to payment details. Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store / Google Play – payment processing (payment data handled by the store). Apple · Google

All processors operate under data processing agreements pursuant to Art. 28 GDPR where applicable.

6) International Transfers

Data may be transferred to third countries depending on the chosen service region. Appropriate safeguards are implemented (Art. 44 et seq. GDPR), such as EU Standard Contractual Clauses and additional protective measures.

7) Data Provision Obligations

Without basic account or content data, core app features cannot be used. You are not required to provide any optional data that is not necessary for functionality.

8) Your Rights

You have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection (Art. 15–21 GDPR). You may also withdraw consent at any time.

You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority, particularly in your member state of residence.
